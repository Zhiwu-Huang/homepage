Research Description

Videos are ideally suited for teaching machines lots of knowledge about the world because they implicitly encode the physical constraints that define it. However, existing computer vision models generally approach the video understanding problems with extremely expensive expert knowledge in terms of data pre-processing, data annotation, feature engineering, network design and loss function exploitation. Our research aims at addressing some of these challenges for both low-level and high-level video understanding problems.
