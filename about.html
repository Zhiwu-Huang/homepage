Short Bio...

Zhiwu Huang is a postdoctoral researcher at ETH Zurich. He pursued his Ph.D. degree at the University of Chinese Academy of Sciences. He obtained his bachelor and master degree from Huaqiao University and Xiamen University respectively. His research interest lies in computer vision and machine learning for automated video artificial intelligence, including video generation, enhancement and manipulation, as well as human-focussed video clustering, classification and prediction. His works focus on automating the learning process of machines to understand the world through videos with few data pre-processing, data annotation, feature engineering, network engineering and loss function design. He serves as reviewers for major conferences and journals in computer vision, machine learning, and artificial intelligence. He has also led the organization of multiple video understanding workshops in conjunction with CVPR, ICCV and ECCV.
